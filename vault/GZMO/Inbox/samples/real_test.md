## Task: Explain the Lorenz Attractor

Explain what a Lorenz attractor is in 3 bullet points. Include sigma, rho, and beta.



---

## GZMO Response
*2026-04-17T17:33:25.353Z*

- The Lorenz attractor is a set of chaotic solutions to the Lorenz system, which describes atmospheric convection.
- It includes three parameters: σ (sigma), ρ (rho), and β (beta).
- These parameters influence the shape and behavior of an attractor that exhibits chaotic dynamics, leading to complex, non-repeating patterns.
