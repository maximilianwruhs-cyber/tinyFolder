## Task: Benchmark

Explain quantum entanglement in 5 bullet points. Be precise and technical.



---

## GZMO Response
*2026-04-18T07:02:52.701Z*

- Quantum entanglement is a phenomenon where pairs or groups of particles interact in such a way that the state of one (whether a spin, vibration mode, etc.) can depend on the state of another.
- Entangled quantum states are non-separable; measurement of one entangled particle instantaneously determines the state of another in correlation.
- Quantum entanglement underpins certain aspects of quantum mechanics, such as superposition and uncertainty relations.
- It defies classical physics' intuition where particles independently evolve without influence from other particles far away.
- Entanglement is a key resource in quantum computing and quantum cryptography protocols.
