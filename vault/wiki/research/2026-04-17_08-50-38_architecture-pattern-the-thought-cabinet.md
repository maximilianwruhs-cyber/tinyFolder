# Research: Architecture Pattern:** The Thought Cabinet employs a stochastic absorption mechanism; submitted thoughts are not immediately guaranteed to be reflected in the `chaos_status` counts.

## Insights

-   **Stochastic Absorption for Noise Filtering & Emergence:** This mechanism probabilistically filters transient inputs, preventing immediate `chaos_status` perturbation. It enables non-linear, emergent shifts
