# The Sedation Paradox — Allostasis and Cognitive Drive Architecture

In computational neuroscience, this directly parallels the **dark room problem** within the Active Inference Framework [2]. Instead, agents must utilize **allostasis**, the continuous process of achieving systemic stability through anticipatory, dynamic change [5, 6]. This anticipatory regulation allows an agent to maintain a **dynamic equilibrium**—which is not a state of rest, but rather a state of continuously and aggressively managed tension capable of adapting to complex, stochastic environments [7, 8].
